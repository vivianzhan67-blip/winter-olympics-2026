Canadian Athletes

Connor McDavid

Connor McDavid is set to make his Olympic debut at the Milano Cortina 2026 Games. He is widely regarded as one of the best hockey players in the world, having won multiple awards, including the Hart Trophy as the NHL's most valuable player.

Natalie Spooner

Spooner is a key player on the Canadian women's hockey team, having won gold at the Sochi 2014 and Beijing 2022 Olympics. She is known for her scoring ability and playmaking skills, making her a crucial asset for Team Canada.

Mikael Kingsbury

Mikael Kingsbury is a dominant figure in mogul skiing, with numerous World Cup victories and multiple world championships to his name. He is a strong contender for medals in the freestyle skiing events at the Olympics.
